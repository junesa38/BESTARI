About Me

Professional sailor, project creator, and problem solver

Who I Am

I'm Asa Putra, a professional sailor with years of experience navigating the world's oceans. When I'm not at sea, I channel my problem-solving skills into creating practical tools and sharing insights through writing.

My journey has taken me from the deck of various vessels to the world of digital creation, where I build calculators, tools, and content that help others solve real-world problems.

What I Do

Maritime Professional

Experienced sailor with expertise in vessel operations, navigation, and marine safety protocols.

Tool Creator

I build practical calculators and tools for maritime, construction, and financial planning needs.

Content Writer

Sharing experiences, insights, and lessons learned from life at sea and on land.

Problem Solver

Combining technical knowledge with practical experience to create solutions that work.

My Philosophy

"The sea teaches you patience, resilience, and the importance of preparation. These lessons apply just as much to life on land as they do to life at sea."

Every project I work on is guided by the principles I've learned during my maritime career: attention to detail, thorough planning, and the understanding that small mistakes can have big consequences.

Current Focus

Currently based in Jakarta, I'm working on expanding my collection of practical tools while continuing my maritime career. I'm particularly interested in creating solutions that bridge the gap between traditional industries and modern technology.

Whether it's calculating fuel consumption for a voyage, estimating construction materials, or making financial decisions about property, I believe in creating tools that are both accurate and easy to use.
